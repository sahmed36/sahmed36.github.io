Freelance Projects:

 

  • Car Repairing Marketplace (www.repairjungle.com):  I've developed this as a platform for mechanics and car owner to collaborate with each other regarding their car repairing issue. Here, car owner can create a task to find a mechanic to repair his/her car and post it. After that, mechanic can bid on that task and win it depending the selection process by the owner. Car owner can rate the mechanic after the task completion which would be visible on the mechanic's profile. Briefly, its a place/platform for car-owner and mechanic to get their task done with an ease.
    Development tools: PHP(Yii framework), MySQL, Javascript, IntelliJ-Idea
